About Vidhisethu

Vidhisethu is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to help users understand the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) - India's criminal law code.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive platform for legal research with three main features:

  • AI Assistant: Describe your legal situation and get relevant BNS sections with AI-powered explanations
  • Section Lookup: Search BNS sections or enter old IPC sections to find their BNS equivalent
  • Case Library: Explore landmark legal cases and judicial precedents

Our Mission

To democratize legal knowledge and make Indian criminal law accessible to everyone.

Technology

Vidhisethu uses RAG, ChromaDB vector database, Sentence Transformers, a local LLM, and Google Maps for semantic search, AI explanations, and location services.
